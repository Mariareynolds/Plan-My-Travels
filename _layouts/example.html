Example Itinerary

Here's a quick example of a 5 day itinerary for Sydney to Byron to give you an example of what you will receive in your full itinerary.

Our itineraries are broken down in to 4 categories:

Intro to a location: Explaining how to get around, travel options and travel cards, hotel suggestions and a location description

Activities: All the things you need to know about the days activity. Including how to get there, what you will be doing and the best places to eat

Tips: Recommended Apps to download to get the best out of your trip including travel apps, happy hour apps and campsite apps.

Travel Directions: Explaining how to get from one location to another using different modes of transport.
