DAY {{ forloop.index }}

Location: Cairns

Activity: Diving the Great Barrier Reef

Whether you have dived before or not you’re on the Great Barrier Reef and you should definitely give it a go. You can book guided dives if your PADI certified or discovery dives if you have never dived before. A discovery dive will cost around $240 for a full day on the reef and you will be able to dive to 6metres and get a feel for life on the reef. Look out for trtles, stingers and possible even some reef sharks. Gairns Diving   For those of you who are experienced divers you can also book full day tours on the reef.
