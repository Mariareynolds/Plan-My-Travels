Location: Gold Coast

Activity: Burleigh Heads and Currumbin National Park

Burleigh Heads is the trendiest spot on the Gold Coast. Beautiful yet quiet beaches and a great atmosphere. Burleigh is a great spot to spend a night and relax mid way through your trip. You can visit Currumbin wildlife centre to cuddle a koala and feed the karangaroo’s for a really reasonable price. Or if you just want to explore you you can visit Currumbin which is a small surf town, again with pretty beaches and coffee shops. In the evening head to Burleigh pavilion for some nightlife.
