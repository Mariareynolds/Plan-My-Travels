Location: Sydney

Activity: Beach Day and Coastal Walk

Bondi to Coogee coastal walk 1.5hours If you’re not pushed for time I’d recommend stopping at a few different beaches and make a day of it. My personal favourite stops are Gordons Bay and Tamara. You’ll also want to do the Bondi Icebergs (sea pools) as it’s a bit of a tourist spot. Bondi and Coogee are the largest of the beaches and you will find restaurants for breakfast or lunch at either end. Coogee Bay beach Hotel has a great atmosphere for afternoon drinks. You will find a cafe at Tamara that does Milkshakes and light lunches or smoothies and fish and chips from the beach hut at Bronte. Coogee Pavilion is a good spot for a night out if you’re looking for a dance floor!
