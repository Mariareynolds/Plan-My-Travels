If you are into your modern art you can visit the Museum of Contemporary Art located at Circular Quay. Opening times are 10:00am – 17:00pm. On Wednesday nights throughout the year, the whole Gallery remains open until 9pm. General Admission is free to the public however popup exhibitions are an additional cost.

The Art Gallery of NSW is one of Australia’s leading art museums. See the collection of Australian, Aboriginal, European, Asian and contemporary art. Located within the Domain Gardens. Admission to the Gallery is free. Temporary exhibitions have a cost. Opening hours, every day 10:00 until 17:00. On Wednesday nights throughout the year, the whole Gallery remains open until 9pm.

The Manly Art Gallery and Museum is the oldest metropolitan regional gallery in NSW and the centre for the visual arts on the Northern Beaches. THe Museum is free entry and opening times are Tuesday – Sunday 10am – 3pm.
