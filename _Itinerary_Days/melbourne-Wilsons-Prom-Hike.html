Wilsons Promontory, known as Wilsons Prom is just under a 3 hour drive from Melbourne via the M1. As this is a National Park there is an entry fee of $9.

Head towards Telegraph Saddle carpark where you will find the start of the 3.4km hike to the top of Mt Oberon for sunset. The hike should take roughly 45minutes to 1 hour. The view from the top is epic especially at sunset.
