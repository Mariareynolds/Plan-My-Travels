Boathaven beach is one of the most recommended in Airlie as it has a stinger net all year round so you can swim with no need to worry and you also have a view of the marina as a backdrop. Boathaven is just a 15 minute walk from the Lagoon and just 8 minutes from the Airlie beach Hotel.

Head to lunch at the Anchor Bar and spend the afternoon at the infinity pool, sipping cocktails with a view of Hook Islands, the largest of all the Whitsundays Islands. The Anchor has a great food and drinks menu and it is a recommended spot for the evening. If you're looking for reasonably priced food then it's a really good option, the make your own cheesecake is very popular!

--- so if you're like me and you love to daydream about which boat will someday be yours then it's a great place to spend the afternoon
