Intro to Canberra

Canberra is Australia's largest inland city and the eighth-largest city overall. Located between Australia's 2 largest cities, Melbourne and Sydney.

Canberra is the capital city of Australia and is home to the nations Parliament house.

Getting Around

After arriving at Canberra airport you can head straight for the Airport bus station to catch a bus in to the City. Buses can vary from 30-45 minutes depending on the route. However if you are happy to pay a little more, especially if travelling in a group you can get a taxi for the roughly 10 minute journey.

Canberra has buses and light rail trains that cover the majority of the City making it really easy to get around. You can either purchase a MyWay Card for $20 with $20 worth of credit or pay by cash for each of your journeys. Purchasing a MyWay Card saves you roughly $1.30 per journey however this may not be worth it if you are not in Canberra for long as when purchasing a cash ticket for $5 it last for 90 minutes which may work out more cost effective than purchasing the MyWay Card for $20.
