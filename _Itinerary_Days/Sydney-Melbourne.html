--- title: Sydney-Melbourne location: Sydney-Melbourne type: direction activity: Travel text: | Flying between Sydney and Melbourne is the most practical way to travel. The flight time is roughly 1hour and 25 minutes and prices can be as low as $70. However in peak seasons prices could reach over $250. As an alternative you could catch a train from Sydney’s Central Station to Melbourne’s Southern Cross station. The journey time is 13hours and prices can range from $65 to $250 also. Greyhound Buses run 3 times a day and have a journey time between 11 and a half and 12 and a half hours. Prices are on average between $60- $200. If you are driving between these states take the M31 inland which should take you just under 9 hours(traffic depending) ---
