Intro to Byron

Byron Bay is the hippie hotspot of the East Coast of Australia and it has loads to offer including, surfing, hidden beaches, boutique shops with beautiful clothes and a care free vibe.

Expect to have adventurous days with chilled evenings listening to the local buskers.

Getting Around

If you are looking to get to Byron from the Coolangatta Airport the Skybus operates every 2 hours with a one way trip at just $28 for the 55minute journey which is much cheaper than any of the taxi's available. It's worth checking the timetable in advance as you may find yourself just missing a shuttle and having to wait 2 hours. Sky Bus
