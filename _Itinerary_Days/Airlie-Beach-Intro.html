Intro to Airlie Beach

Airlie Beach is the vibrant hub of the Whitsundays and the ideal mainland base for fun and adventure in the Whitsundays. Located on the Great Barrier Reef and known for it's young and adventurous atmosphere it is the perfect launching pad for activities on the water and in the lush, green rainforest of the Whitsunday Coast. If you're looking to explore the Whitsunday Islands without the expense of staying on the Islands then this is definitely the place for you.

Getting Around

If you have flown in to Proserpine Airport you will find plenty of shuttle buses that can transport you to Airlie Beach. Many of these need to be rebooked so it’s recommended to book a shuttle before your flight. You can book a one way pick up for $20 or a $34 return with Airlie Beach Transfers

Whitsundays Transit is the local bus service connecting Proserpine, Cannonvale, Airlie Beach and Shute Harbour. Airlie beach is a small town where everything is accessible on foot. However some accommodation are located on fairly steep hills and if you don’t fancy walking there are plenty of cheap taxis in the area that will cost no more the $12 to get you across town.
