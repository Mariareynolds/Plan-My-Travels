Head to the Botanical Gardens from the City, it’s roughly a 17 minute walk from Central station. Make a stop at Coles or Woolworths on the way to grab some snacks for a picnic in the park. If you are visiting on a Sunday the Botanical gardens has riverside markets between 8am - 3pm so you may be able to grab some treats here too.

Relax in the sun and enjoy a walk around the park before heading to Howard Smith Wharves for a late afternoon drink. Howard Smith Wharves has 12 different restaurants and bars to choose from. Pick a riverside location to enjoy the evening atmosphere before moving on to the nightlife in Fortitude Valley.

Depending on how the night goes you’ll find yourself either boogying in a nightclub or sipping cocktails in a rooftop bar. So take your pick from some of the options below.
