--- title: Adelaide-Melbourne location: Adelaide-Melbourne type: direction activity: Travel text: | If you have a car you can drive from Adelaide to Melbourne via the famous Great Ocean Road. The total journey is roughly 11 and a half hours. If you have time to spare or if you already planned to visit the Great Ocean Road it’s worth stopping in Warnambool overnight to break up the journey and enjoy a slower drive along the Great Ocean Road the following day. If you’re looking for a shorter journey you can take the inland route which is roughly an 8 hour drive. If you’re on a tight time frame you should fly from Adelaide to Melbourne, it’s a 1 hour and 15 minute flight and can be as cheap as $60 one way. You can catch a train from Adelaide to Melbourne changing at Nhill from just $114. The journey time is roughly 11hours from start to finish. ---
