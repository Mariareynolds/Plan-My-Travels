--- title: melbourne-Zoo img: /images/.JPG img_alt: location: Melbourne type: activity activity: Melbourne Zoo text: | The Melbourne Zoo is open every day from 09:00 – 17:00. Trains run to Royal Park Station on the Upfield Line. Take Tram 55 which runs from William St in the city and stops in front of the zoo. Tickets are $38 and can be purchased online or on the day. You can also book up close experiences with Gorilla’s, Giraffe’s and even the Tigers. The Zoo has 3 cafe’s onsite so you can enjoy, The Lakeside Cafe, the Elephant Cafe and the Giraffe Lookout Cafe. ---
