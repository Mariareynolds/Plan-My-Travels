--- title: melbourne-Brighton-Bathing-Boxes img: /images/.JPG img_alt: location: Melbourne type: activity activity: Brighton Bathing Boxes text: | The Brighton Bathing boxes often feature in Melbourne’s Tourism campaigns so it’s worth heading out for the City for a day. A train from the city will take around 20 minutes on the Sandringham line. Catch it from Flinders Street Station to Brighton Beach Railway Station and take the scenic 500-metre stroll down to the multi-coloured bathing boxes. The Baths, Middle Brighton has a deck overlooking the beach and has a great breakfast/lunch menu swell as cocktails and milkshakes. There is also kiosk if you just wanted to grab a coffee and a snack as you walk along the promenade. ---
