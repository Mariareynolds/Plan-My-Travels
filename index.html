What to expect

Plan My Travels offers bespoke itineraries, designed around the length of time you plan on travelling, your travelling style preferences and the locations you select. You will receive a link to your own personalised login page detailing your trip day by day. It’s as simple as that. If you have more questions you are welcome you use the additional comments box when submitting your travel preferences for a quote and we will come back to you to help your with your plans as best as we can.

Why use Plan My Travels

Are you looking to travel a country you know nothing about? Starting a trip with no ideal start or finishing point? Or are you simply working long hours and don’t have the time to do your research but you also don’t want to miss out on anything. Plan my travels was born to fill that void. This website will create custom itineraries for your trip based on the locations you’d like to visit, how long you’re staying and you personal interests. Answer just 9 short questions to allow us to customise a bespoke itinerary that will not only point out where you should visit during your stay but also how to get from A to B and also a few tips on restaurants and hotels along the way. All destinations have been personally visited by the founder of Plan My Travels meaning these locations have been hand picked and are considered worthy of a visit.

Who is behind the scenes

What do we do?

Plan my travels explores destinations all over Australia (and more countries in the pipeline) showing off the best of each location. Once you have selected which locations appeal to you you will submit your preferences and will then be sent a full itinerary for your own trip.
